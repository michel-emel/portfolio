About Me

Hi there, my name is Michel Emel.

I am a Junior Data Engineer, Data Scientist, and Machine Learning Engineer with a strong mathematical foundation. My passion lies in transforming data into actionable insights to drive innovative solutions and informed decision-making.

My hands-on experience spans the entire data lifecycle, from ingestion and preprocessing to model building and deployment. I am proficient in Python, SQL, NoSQL, R, AppScript, and JavaScript, and skilled in using Apache Hadoop, Spark, Airflow, Databricks, and Snowflake. I design scalable data pipelines on GCP, AWS, and Azure, and work with Docker, Kubernetes, and related tools.

In machine learning, I use TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, PyTorch, and Keras, along with techniques like XGBoost, LightGBM and related tools. My expertise includes deploying LLMs and creating data visualizations with Tableau, Power BI, Looker, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Plotly. I manage databases such as PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB, and Redis, and am experienced in API integration and business intelligence with REST.

My mathematical skills enhance my algorithm development, statistical analysis, and model optimization. Committed to continuous learning, I stay updated with industry trends to deliver high-quality results. I am excited to tackle new challenges and advance my career in data engineering, data science, and machine learning.
